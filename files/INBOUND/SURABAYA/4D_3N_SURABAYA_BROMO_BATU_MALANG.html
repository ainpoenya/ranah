4D/3N BROMO – BATU/MALANG - SURABAYA

Day 01  : SURABAYA – BROMO TOSARI (L/D)
Arrive at Juanda Airport Surabaya. Meet & greet by our tour guide. Have Surabaya Discovery tour, visit : Submarine  Monument (Monkasel), a real KRI Pasopati 410 Whiskey Class Russian Submarine served in the Indonesia Navy since 1952, and since her decommission in 1990, it is now preserved as a monument. Next visit Heroes Monument (Tugu Pahlawan) which dedicated to the people who died during "the Battle of Surabaya" on 10 November 1945, we also visit the Museum 10 November which houses a collection of weapons wield by the heroes of Surabaya. Stop by at The House of Sampoerna, situated in "old Surabaya", this stately Dutch Colonial style building was built in 1962 and now preserved as a historical site. Behind the building is clove cigarette factory where you can witness hundreds of worker girls hand-rolling "kretek" at incredible speed.  Before we go to Mt. Bromo we will stop at  Cheng Ho Mosque, it is the first mosque built in the city based on traditional looks no different than a Buddhist or Taoist Temple. Transfer to Mt. Bromo (+/-  3,5 hour drive by car/coach). Dinner will be served at local restaurant. Check into Bromo Cottage Hotel at Tosari area.

Day 02  : BROMO TOSARI – BATU/MALANG (B/L/D)
Early morning call at 03.00 hours, in time for the highlight of your tour, watching the glorious sunrise and the spectacular painting view scene of Mount Bromo and its surrounding vista. Depart by local transport or four-wheel drive jeep to Mount Penanjakan, view platform point 1 located at 2750 meters above the sea level. From this vintage platform you will have the perfect view of watching the spectacular sunrise and also the serene view of Mt. Bromo at a distance. Thereafter, drive down to the sea of sand, where you will continue by horseback riding across the sea of sand up to Mt Bromo. Walkup 250 steps to reach the rim of the crater of Mt Bromo located 2300 meter above sea level. The Sunrise Scene and surrounding vista from this point is a tremendous spectacle. Drive back to hotel for breakfast & shower. Check out from hotel then transfer to Batu Highland, a mountain resort region for overnight. Have lunch at local restaurant. Visit Transport Museum (Museum Angkut) & Movie Star Studio, afterward visit Batu Floating Market. Overnight at Batu / Malang. Dinner at local restaurant.

Day 03 : MALANG/BATU – SIDOARJO - SURABAYA (B/L/D)
In the morning you will be picked up by our tour guide then visit The apple plantation at Batu Highland (visitors allowed to pick up the fruits at this plantation). Transfer to Sidoarjo City. Shopping at Tanggulangin which known for their leather products with reasonable prices (such as belts, purses, bags, shoes). Continue to buy “Oleh-oleh” at Sidoarjo Shrimp Crackers which have so many traditional snacks of Indonesia. Have lunch at local restaurant. Transfer to Surabaya City. Visit Mosque & Tomb of Sunan Ampel , one of the member of Walisongo -  the nine saints - who spread the moslem religion in Java at 14th Century , you may also have your chance to shop in this Arab street. Last visit to Mirota Shop & Batik Gallery for buying souvenirs. Overnight at Surabaya

Day 04 : SURABAYA – TRANSFER OUT (B)
After breakfast at hotel, last minute shopping at jembatan Merah Plaza / Tunjungan Plaza before transfer to Surabaya airport for next flight.
