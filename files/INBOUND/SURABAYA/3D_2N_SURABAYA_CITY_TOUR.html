3D/2N SURABAYA CITY HOLIDAYS PACKAGE

Day 01 : SURABAYA – DISCOVERY TOURS (L/D)
Arrive at Juanda Airport Surabaya. Meet & greet by our tour guide. Have Surabaya Discovery tour, visit : Submarine  Monument (Monkasel), a real KRI Pasopati 410 Whiskey Class Russian Submarine served in the Indonesia Navy since 1952, and since her decommission in 1990, it is now preserved as a monument. Next visit Heroes Monument (Tugu Pahlawan) which dedicated to the people who died during "the Battle of Surabaya" on 10 November 1945, we also visit the Museum 10 November which houses a collection of weapons wield by the heroes of Surabaya. Stop by at The House of Sampoerna, situated in "old Surabaya", this stately Dutch Colonial style building was built in 1962 and now preserved as a historical site. Behind the building is clove cigarette factory where you can witness hundreds of worker girls hand-rolling "kretek" at incredible speed.  Next visit Surabaya Zoo where we can see the famous Komodo Dragon the world's largest lizard. After that check in hotel. Dinner at local restaurant.

Day 02    : SURABAYA –  SHOPPING TOURS (B/L/D)
Breakfast at Hotel. First have shopping at Mirota Art Shop & Batik Gallery. Afterward visit  Mosque & Tomb of Sunan Ampel, the oldest mosque in Surabaya, built by Raden Rahmatulllah in 1421. It tell a special story on Sunan Ampel, one of the member of Walisongo (the nine saints) which spread Islam in Java, you will also have the time to shop at this Arab Quarter. Next visit  Cheng Ho Mosque, it is the first mosque built in the city based on traditional looks no different than a Buddist or Taoist Temple. Transfer to Sidoarjo City for shopping stops at Tanggulangin, known for their leather products at very reasonable prices, stop at  Mitra Shop for its prawn & fish crackers and many other traditional local snacks, afterward visit Indah Embroidery Moslem Fashion store.  Back to Surabaya city. Last visit to Tunjungan Plaza Shopping Mall or Grand City Mall. Return to your hotel & overnight.

Day 03    : SURABAYA – TRANSFER OUT (B)
After breakfast Free at your own leisure until departure time you will be picked up by our tour guide then transfer to Surabaya airport.
