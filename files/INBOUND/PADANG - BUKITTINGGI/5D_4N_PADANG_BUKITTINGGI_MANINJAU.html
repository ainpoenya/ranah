5D 4N PADANG - BUKITTINGGI – MANINJAU

Day 01 : AIRPORT - MANINJAU (L/D)
Upon arrival at Minangkabau International Airport, meeting and greeting service by our representative.Then proceed to Bukit tinggi, and stop over at Anai Waterfall. After lunch another brief stop will be made at Pandai Sikek, a village famous for weaving and wood-carving. Visit Kota Gadang and Lake Maninjau through 44 hairpin curve road to enjoy the beautiful scenery of the lake. Continue to Museum Rumah kelahiran Buya Hamka. Check in to hotel for overnight stay. Dinner will be provided at the local restaurant.

Day 02 : MANINJAU - BUKIT TINGGI (B/L/D)
After breakfast at the hotel, proceed with City-sightseeing Tour of Bukittinggi visiting the famous Grand Sianok Canyon with the most spectacular view of the surrounding with the Japanese Tunnel incorporated within the area. Stop over at Jam Gadang and Pasar Atas (Upper Market). Lunch will be provided at the local restaurant. In the afternoon drive back to
Bukit tinggi and free at leisure. Dinner will be provided at the local restaurant.

Day 03 : BUKIT TINGGI - MINANGKABAU (B/L/D)
After breakfast at the hotel, proceed to En route, stop at Desa Baso (Hj. Rosma), stop over at Tabek Patah, a look out point with panoramic view of the beautiful landscape, visiting the local home-industry such as Pisang Salai and the traditional coffee milling. Visit Pagaruyung King Palace and lunch will be provided at the local restaurant which is surrounded by the magnificent rice/paddy field. After lunch will visit Lake Singkarak and drive back to Bukit Tinggi. Free at leisure and dinner will be provided at the local restaurant.

Day 04 : BUKITTINGGI SHOPPING TOUR – PADANG (B/L/D)
After breakfast at the hotel, shopping at Aur Kuning. After lunch drive to Padang. On arrival in Padang, proceeds with City – sightseeing Tour visiting China Town, Old Harbor, Siti Nurbaya Bridge, Padang Beach and free program for shopping until the time to drop you off at the hotel. Dinner will be provided at the local restaurant.

Day 05: PADANG - AIRPORT ( B )
After breakfast, check out from hotel and drop to the airport for your flight home or next destination.
