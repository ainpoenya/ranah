5D/4N JAKARTA – PUNCAK - BANDUNG + TANGKUBAN PERAHU TOUR

DAY 01 : JAKARTA ARRIVAL - PUNCAK (L,D)
Upon arrival at Soekarno Hatta Airport Jakarta, meet and greet with our guide and starting to Jakarta City Tour visiting MONAS, RUMAH BATIK and TAMAN MINI INDONESIA INDAH. In the afternoon, proceed to PUNCAK for overnight and Stop over at MASDJID BESAR in Puncak Tea Mountain Resort.  Lunch and Dinner will serve at local restuarant.

DAY 02 : PUNCAK - BANDUNG (B/L/D)
After breakfast at hotel, today programs proceed to Bandung around 2 – 3 Hours journey via Cianjur. on the way stop over shopping at Cianjur Snacks & Crackers. arrive at Bandung, visit TANGKUBAN PERAHU MOUNTAIN (WEEKDAY) and FLOATING MARKET. After dinner at local halal restaurant, check in hotel and free program.

DAY 03 : BANDUNG FULLDAY SHOPPING (B,L,D)
After breakfast at hotel, full day shopping at CIHAMPELAS JEANS CENTER, FACTORY OUTLET and shopping at CIBADUYUT SHOES CENTER to buy home industry handicrafts from leather (bags, shoes, slipper, etc). Lunch and Dinner will serve at local restaurant

DAY 04 : BANDUNG - JAKARTA (B,L,D)          
After breakfast at hotel, shopping at PASAR BARU WHOLESELLER MARKET for buying Kain Batik, Silk, Baju Kurung, Telekung, Sarong and Local food products like Tempe Oncom, sale Pisang, Keripik, etc and TOKO TIGA. In the afternoon, back to Jakarta for Overnight. Lunch and Dinner will serve at local restuarant

DAY 05 : JAKARTA DEPARTURE (B)
After breakfast at hotel, Last Minute Shopping at MANGGA DUA SHOPPING COMPLEX or THAMRIN CITY MALL before transfer to the airport
