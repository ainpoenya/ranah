3D/2N MEDAN LAKE TOBA TOUR

DAY 1 : ARRIVAL MEDAN – PARAPAT (L,D)
Arrive at Kuala Namu International Airport Medan , greeting service with Tour Guide. Stop at Licenced  money changer. Transfer to local restaurant for lunch. After lunch proceed to Parapat Lake Toba. Enroute stop at Patent Shop for buying local food products named Teng Teng , Ting Ting , Etc. Arrive at Parapat check in to Hotel for accommodation. Dinner at Hotel

DAY 2 : PARAPAT – SAMOSIR ISLAND – PARAPAT – BERASTAGI (B,L,D)
After breakfast at Hotel , cruise the Lake Toba by Ferry Boat about 45 minutes for visiting Tomok Village to see the old grave stone of King Sidabutar , continued to visit Ambarita Village to see the old chairs stone and execution place made by King Siallagan. Back to Parapat transfer to local restaurant for lunch. After lunch tour continued to Berastagi. Enroute stop at Simarjarunjung to enjoy Ginger Tea and Pisang Goreng (Personal account). Stop at Sipiso Piso waterfall to see the waterfall about 110 meter and to see the extraordinary view of Lake Toba from Northern. Proceed to Berastagi upon arrival at Berastagi visit Central Fruits Market for buying tropical fruits , vegetables and flowers. Check in to Hotel , dinner at Hotel.

DAY 3 : BERASTAGI – MEDAN – AIRPORT (B)
Breakfast at Hotel back to Medan via Sibolangit and Sembahe about 2 hours drive. Arrive in Medan visit Maimoon Palace, Grand Mosque then transfer to Airport for your next destinations
