3D/2N LOMBOK – GILI ISLAND

 Day 1  : Arrival -  Lombok -  Sasak Tour ( L,D )
Arrive at Lombok International Airport, Meet and great with local guide, starting tour visiting sasak tour visiting traditional market, Banyumulek pottery village, Sukarara hand weaving village, Sade / Rambitan Sasak traditional houses, Kuta and Tanjung A'an beach then transfer to hotel free at your leisure time. Lunch and dinner will be serve at local rest.

Day 2   : Gili Trawangan + Snorkeling (B,L,D )
Breakfast at hotel then start the tour with a visit to Kekait traditional market and Sidemen palm sugar village. Then proceed the trip to Pusuk preserved Monkey forest from where the beautiful rice paddy field near Bangsal harbor can be seen. Ride the boat to Gili Trawangan to enjoy the underwater view of a fantastic turquoise beach. In the afternoon drive back to hotel through Malimbu hill to enjoy the breathtaking scenery of Gili Air, Meno, and Trawangan. Lunch and Dinner will be served at local restaurant.

Day 3   : Departure – Senggigi Beach Tour ( B )
Breakfast at hotel, free program, visiting Senggigi beach and transfer to Airport

 

3D/2 LOMBOK SUN BATHING

Day 1   : Arrival -  Lombok -  Sasak Tour ( L,D )
Arrive at Lombok International Airport, Meet and great with local guide, starting tour visiting sasak tour visiting traditional market, Banyumulek pottery village, Sukarara hand weaving village, Sade / Rambitan Sasak traditional houses, Kuta and Tanjung A'an beach then transfer to hotel free at your leisure time. Lunch and dinner will be serve at local restaurant.

Day 2   : Gili Nanggu Tour ( B,L,D )
Gili Nanggu tour begin at 8:00, the trip will start from your hotel and then transfer to Tawun port which will spend about a half hour drive. On the way to Gili Nanggu, you can enjoy the scenic beauty of the island of Lombok as Sekotong village and Lembar Harbor. From Tawun port, we will spend about 20 minute’s journey to the Gili Nanggu by boats that have been modified. On Gili Nanggu you can spend time for relaxing, swimming, snorkeling, and sun bathing. You can enjoy the scenic beauty in Gili Nanggu until 15:30 and then we drive back to Lombok. Lunch will be done at Gili Nanggu or Gili Sudak. On the way back to the hotel, we will visit Labuapi village as the biggest wood carving in Lombok, and dinner at local restaurant.

Day 3   : Departure – Senggigi Beach Tour ( B )
Breakfast at hotel, free program, visiting Senggigi beach and transfer to Airport
