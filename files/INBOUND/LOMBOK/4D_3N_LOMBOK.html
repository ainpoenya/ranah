4D/3N LOMBOK EXOTIC

Day 1  : Lombok Arrival – City Tour ( L,D )
Arrive at Lombok International Airport, Meet and great with local guide, than transfer to local restaurant for Lunch. After That, city tour where this tour will visit Mayura water palace was built in 1744. The floating hall built in the middle of the pond used to function as court of justice in kingdom time. The compound also has an active shrine for Hindu worshippers living around the city of Mataram. Narmada temple complex was built in 1727 to replicate the highest and holiest mount Rinjani, where the king used to climb to for offering. Other highlights are Fountain of Youth and lake replica. Lingsar is the holiest temple in Lombok and is built in 1714 with the help of local Sasak Moslem which is often referred to as Wetu Telu. Therefore in the compound, a shrine of Kemaliq is also built for Wetu Telu to symbolize the unity. After Dinner, transfer to Hotel for check in and free at leisure.

Day 2   : Gili Trawangan + snorkeling (B,L,D )
Breakfast at hotel then start the tour with a visit to Kekait traditional market and Sidemen palm sugar village. Then proceed the trip to Pusuk preserved Monkey forest from where the beautiful rice paddy field near Bangsal harbor can be seen. Ride the boat to Gili Trawangan to enjoy the underwater view of a fantastic turquoise beach. In the afternoon drive back to hotel through Malibu hill to enjoy the breathtaking scenery of Gili Air, Meno, and Trawangan. Lunch and Dinner will be served at local restaurant.

Day 03 : Fullday Sasak Tour (B,L,D)
After breakfast at hotel, starting for sasak tour, where this tour will visit Sade remains the most interesting visit for those who are in search for tribal attraction. It is a Sasak compound built on slightly hilly area featuring traditional houses. Meet and greet the local at their own village at Sade is awesome. Kuta & An Beaches are perfect paradise for surfers and an ideal place to sunbath. Swimming is also good during high tide in the turquoise crystal clear water. Otherwise, simply walk on the granular strikingly white sand bare feet to feel natural foot reflection. Sukarare offers weaving experience to visitors as the weavers glad to invite them to try the traditional loom. The fabric woven here represents the art of clothing of the indigenous. Songket and ikat weaving are amongst the most famous features. Lunch and Dinner will serve at local restaurant.

Day 4   : Departure – Senggigi Beach Tour (B)
Breakfast at hotel, free program, visiting Senggigi beach and transfer to Airport.
