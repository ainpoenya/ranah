4D3N LOMBOK HONEYMOON PACKAGE

DAY 01 : LOMBOK ARRIVAL – CITY TOUR (L,D)
Arrive at Lombok International Airport, Meet and great with local guide, and transfer to Local restaurant for Lunch. After that, starting to City Tour visiting Visit MUSEUM MATARAM, Narmada temple complex was built in 1727 to replicate the highest and holiest mount Rinjani, where the king used to climb to for offering. Other highlights are Fountain of Youth and lake replica. Lingsar is the holiest temple in Lombok and is built in 1714 with the help of local Sasak Moslem which is often referred to as Wetu Telu. Therefore in the compound, a shrine of Kemaliq is also built for Wetu Telu to symbolize the unity. After Dinner at local restaurant, transfer to hotel for check in.

DAY 02 : GILI TRAWANGAN TOUR (B,L,D)
Breakfast at hotel, then start the tour with a visit to Kekait traditional market and Sidemen palm sugar village. Then proceed the trip to Pusuk preserved Monkey forest from where the beautiful rice paddy field near Bangsal harbor can be seen. Ride the boat to Gili Trawangan to enjoy the underwater view of a fantastic turquoise beach. In the afternoon drive back to Senggigi through Malibu hill to enjoy the breathtaking scenery of Gili Air, Meno, and Trawangan. And then Traditional Body Massage for 60 Minutes. Candle Light Dinner will be served at local restaurant / Hotel.

DAY 03 : FULLDAY SASAK TOUR (B,L,D)
After breakfast and chek out hotel, join with the next touring that we call Sasak tour, where this tour will visit : Banyumulek is the most visited pottery village on the island for its beautiful and unique designs. The potters work in traditional ways which they learn from their ancestors. Many of their masterpieces have been exported to many countries. Sukarare offers weaving experience to visitors as the weavers glad to invite them to try the traditional loom. The fabric woven here represents the art of clothing of the indigenous. Songket and ikat weaving are amongst the most famous features. Sade remains the most interesting visit for those who are in search for tribal attraction. It is a Sasak compound built on slightly hilly area featuring traditional houses. Meet and greet the local at their own village at Sade is awesome. Kuta & Tamjung A'an Beaches are perfect paradise for surfers and an ideal place to sunbath. Swimming is also good during high tide in the turquoise crystal clear water. Otherwise, simply walk on the granular strikingly white sand bare feet to feel natural foot reflection.(Lunch and Dinner at Local Resto).

DAY 04 : DEPARTURE (B)
After breakfast at hotel, last Minute shopping for Buying Oleh – Oleh From Lombok Such as Mutiara, Lombok Exotic T-Shirt, etc before transfer to airport for next flight.
