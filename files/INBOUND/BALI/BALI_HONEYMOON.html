3D/2N BALI HONEYMOON PACKAGE

DAY 01 : BALI ARRIVAL (D)
Upon arrival at Ngurahrai Airport, meet and great with our Tour Guide and trip continue to photo Balinese Costum, after that continue to Uluwatu Temple located at the southhernmost of Bali catch a wonderful view as the sunset into deep blue ocean. Candle Light Dinner will serve at Jimbaran

DAY 02 : KINTAMANI TOUR (B,L,D)
After Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed to Batubulan village to see the Barong dance ( a Dance representing of the enternal fighting between good and evil ) then continue to visit Batik producing center, wood carving silver and gold jewelry, painting and other handicraft center, after that direct to Kintamani to see the beautiful panoramic of Mt. lake Batur. Lunch will be served at Kintamani Restaurant Buffet set Menu, after lunch continue to visit Tegallalang village see the rice terrace in the late of afternoon Back to Kuta and Dinner at Local restaurant, transfer to hotel

DAY 03 : SPA TREATMENT – TRANSFER OUT (B,L)
After breakfast at hotel proceed to Spa Treatment 2 Hrs, Lunch local Restaurant and Transfer to Airport
