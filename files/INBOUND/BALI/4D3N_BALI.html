4D/3N BALI EXCOTIC - 1

Day 1 : Arrival Bali - Uluwatu Tour (L,D)
Upon arrival at airport ngurah rai bali, meeting service with our local guide, then proceed lunch at local restaurant. After lunch continue to visit pendawa Beach at Kutuh Village and Uluwatu temple see the sunset. After Dinner at BBQ Seafood Jimbaran, transfer to Hotel for check in. Free Program.

Day 2 : Volcano - Traditional Village Tour (B,L,D)
After Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed to Batubulan village to see the Barong dance (a Dance representing of the enternal fighting between good and evil). then continue to visit Batik producing center, wood carving silver and gold jewelry painting and other handicraft center. After that direct to Kintamani to see the beautiful panoramic of Mt. lake Batur.
After Lunch at Kintamani Restaurant, continue to visit Tegallalang village see the rice terrace and Shopping at Tranditional Market Pasar Sukowati. In the afternoon, back to Kuta and Dinner at Local restaurant.

Day 3 : Bedugul - Tanah Lot Tour (B,L,D)
After Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed to Taman Ayun Temple at Mengwi, fruit market in Candi Kuning Bedugul, Ulun Danu Temple. After Lunch at Local restaurant, visit Alas Kedaton Monkey Forest and the last visiting Tanah Lot temple see the sunset. After Dinner at local restaurant, back to Hotel.

Day 4 : Departure (B)
After Breakfast at Hotel, Last Minute Shopping at Kaos Joger and Pusat Oleh – Oleh Krisna before transfer to airport.
