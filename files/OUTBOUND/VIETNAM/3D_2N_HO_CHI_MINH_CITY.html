3D 2N HO CHI MINH CITY – CU CHI TOUR

Day 1: HOME – HCMC (D)
Upon arrival at Tan Son Nhat International Airport, warmest reception by our representative then transfer to hotel check-in. Free time for shopping and relaxation in city center
Have dinner at Halal restaurant. Free time for visit Ben Thanh night market at night
Overnight in HCMC.

Day 2: HCMC – CU CHI – HCMC (B-L-D)
After Halal breakfast, transfer to Cu Chi and visit Cu Chi Tunnel where human knowledge has been enriched by experiencing a web-like tunnel system of more than 200km in length which provide shelters for Vietnamese guerrillas during the Vietnam War against American troops.
Afterwards, go back to HCMC. Have lunch at Halal restaurant in HCMC.
After lunch enjoy city tour in HCMC, the biggest and busiest city of Vietnam, with visits The Hundred Years Red Cathedral (Notre Dame Cathedral), The Old Post Office and The People’s Committee Hall which were all built during the French colonial period. Then visit the War Museum, famous lacquer ware factory and free time for shopping at the Ben Thanh market, Vincom Mall, Parkson Center and many shops along Dong Khoi, Nguyen Hue street. Have dinner at local Halal restaurant. Overnight in HCMC

Day 3: HCMC –  DEPARTURE (B)
After Halal breakfast, visit Ar-Rahim Mosque-built in 1885 by Malaysian-Indonesian Muslim.
More than 200 people pray here OR Free time for more shopping (If time permitting)
until transfer to airport for departure.
