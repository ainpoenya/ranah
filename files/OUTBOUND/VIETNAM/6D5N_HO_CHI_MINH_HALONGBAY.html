6 DAYS / 5 NIGHTS HO CHI MINH (HCMC) – MY THO – HANOI – HALONG BAY

Day 1: HOME – HCMC (D)
Upon arrival at Tan Son Nhat International Airport, warmest reception by our representative then transfer to hotel for check-in. Free time for shopping and relaxation in HCMC.
Have Halal Dinner at local restaurant.
Visit Ben Thanh night market.
Overnight in HCMC.

Day 2: HCMC – CU CHI TUNNELS (B-L-D)
After breakfast, transfer to Cu Chi and visit Cu Chi Tunnel where human knowledge has been enriched by experiencing a web-like tunnel system of more than 200km in length which provide shelters for Vietnamese guerrillas during the Vietnam War against American troops. Afterwards, go back to HCMC. Have lunch at local restaurant in HCMC. After lunch enjoy city tour in HCMC, the biggest and busiest city of Vietnam, with visits The Hundred Years Red Cathedral (Notre Dame Cathedral), The Old Post Office and The People’s Committee Hall which were all built during the French colonial period. Then visit the Reunification Palace, the War Museum, famous lacquer ware factory and shopping at the Ben Thanh market for great.
Enjoy Halal dinner at local restaurant in HCMC
Overnight in HCMC

Day 3: HCMC – MY THO – HCMC (B-L-D)
After breakfast, transfer to My Tho with the colorful outskirt life as well as picturesque rice fields along the journey. Arrive My Tho and get on a boat trip up Mekong River with a stop at the Thoi Son islet to taste seasonal fruits, enjoy traditional folk songs. Visit the authentic life of the local and challenge your bargaining power at cottage handicraft shops. Enjoy an adventurous sampan ride in palm shading creeks. Then take the big boat to visit Hong Van Garden, visit bee farm, taste coconut candy, and drink tea – honey – lime here. And then, transfer back to HCMC. Have Halal lunch at local restaurant.
Free time for shopping and relaxation in HCMC such as: Ben Thanh Market, Vincom Mall, Parkson
Center and many shops along Nguyen Hue, Le Loi street
Enjoy Halal dinner at local restaurant.
Overnight in HCMC

Day 4: HCMC – HALONG BAY (B-L-D)
After breakfast, transfer to the airport for flight to Hanoi. Upon arrival, transfer directly to Halong Bay,
a world natural heritage site since 1994. Then take a beautiful boat trip in Halong Bay to view multi-shaped rocks and visit the dramatic cave Paradise Grotto.
A great seafood lunch will be served on boat. Later in the after noon, check in a hotel, enjoy seafood dinner at a local restaurant.
Evening walk to Halong night market.
Overnight in Halong Bay.

Day 5: HALONG – HANOI (B-L-D)
After early breakfast, check out the hotel and transfer to Hanoi, stop at Dong Trieu ceramic village and Hong Ngoc handicraft center
Upon arrival in Hanoi, have Halal lunch, then visit the Temple of Literature (The 1st University of Vietnam) – the present symbol of Hanoi, the Ngoc Son Temple, the Old Quarter of 36 guild streets through the hustle and bustle of the city center. Enjoy walking and shopping on the Silk street, the Gold and Silver street, Dinh Liet street, Ma May street, Dong Xuan market, etc. Check in at a hotel.
Enjoy Halal dinner at a local restaurant.
Overnight in Hanoi

Day 6: HANOI – DEPARTURE (B-L)
After breakfast, check out the hotel, take Hanoi city tour covering the Ba Dinh Square where most of national events take place, visit the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum & his residence in a well-kept garden, the One Pillar Pagoda – the former symbol of Hanoi and enjoy more shopping in the Old Quarter.
After lunch, enjoy some late shopping till time for transfer to the airport for departure.
End of service
