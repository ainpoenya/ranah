5D4N BANGKOK - PATTAYA


Day 01 : BANGKOK ARRIVAL (D)

  1. Arrival to Bangkok, Meet with our Local Guide, Dinner at Local Muslim Restaurant and Check in Hotel.

Day 02 : BANGKOK CITY TOUR (B/L/D)

  1. B'Fast at hotel, Tour Chaopraya River (Cross by Boat Only) & Wat Arun Temple,
  2. Visit Wat Pho (Reclining Buddha), DS Leather Factory,
  3. Lunch at Sara Restaurant Halal Buffet Nouvo City Hotel with Sholat at hotel.
  4. Visit Magic Arts (3 Dimensi Thai Traditional Photo) and Shopping at MBK Shopping Mall.
  5. Special Dinner at Nopparat Restaurant (Set Thai Food Menu with Thai Traditional Dancing or Buffet Suki) Halal Food.
  6. Visit Asiatique River Front Night Market and Back hotel.

Day 03 : BANGKOK - PATTAYA (B/L/D)

  1. B'Fast at hotel, Transfer to Pattaya, Visit Silverlake Grape Farm,
  2. Tour Nong Nuch Village with Buffet Lunch and Sholat at Masjid Darul Ibadah Pattaya.
  3. Visit SK Honey Bee Product or Super Bee Product, Dinner Halal Buffet at A-One Royal Cruise Hotel and Check in hotel.

Day 04 : PATTAYA - BANGKOK (B/L/D)

  1. B'Fast at hotel, Visit Gems Gallery (World's Biggest Jewelry Store),
  2. Transfer to Bangkok, Visit Chanika Thai Dried Food Market, Lunch at Jan Siaw Restaurant and Sholat at Masjid Al-Alawi.
  3. Shopping at Platinum Shopping Mall, Dinner at Local Muslim Restaurant and Check in Hotel.

Day 05 : BANGKOK DEPARTURE (B)

  1. B'Fast at hotel, Visit Thai Chocolate House, Transfer to Airport for Check in Flight.
